 The Formula SAE competition is an engineering design competition to design and produce a prototype race car for prospective investors.

 Winning Formula.
There are several different aspects of competition, including dynamic events, business presentations, design analysis, and cost reports.

While the ultimate goal of the season is producing a competitive race car, there are many more aspects of the project than simply design and production.
